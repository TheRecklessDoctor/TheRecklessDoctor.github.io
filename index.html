Global Population Statistics

This is an analysis and visualization of data relating to population changes around the globe. The data ranges over a 52 year span starting from 1970 to 2022. Various charts have been used to see how human populations changed, based on the continents. First, let us look at how the world population looked like in the year 1970. One look tells us two major things, that the developed world was still suffering the effects of the world wars and the mass migration of the late 20th and 21st centuries had not yet begun.

As explained above, we can see the clear contrasts between the world in 1970 vs the world in 2022, countries like Russia have more or less recovered from the devastating 20th century and the South Asian regions have begun to expand rapidly in population terms.
